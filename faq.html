What materials are your seat covers made from? Our seat covers are crafted from the right ingredients. We use high-quality materials, including genuine neoprene, leather, faux leather, canvas-cordura, camouflage, sheepskin, saddleblanket, tweed, upholstery grade cloths and more!

- **Are your seat covers waterproof?**
- Some of our seat covers are designed to be waterproof or water-resistant, providing exceptional protection.
- **Are your seat covers custom-made for specific car models?**
- Absolutely, we offer both universal-fit and tailor-made seat covers designed to perfectly match specific car makes and models.
- **How do I determine which seat cover size fits my vehicle?**

- Indeed, we provide seat covers for rear seats, including versatile bench seats, along with tailored solutions.
